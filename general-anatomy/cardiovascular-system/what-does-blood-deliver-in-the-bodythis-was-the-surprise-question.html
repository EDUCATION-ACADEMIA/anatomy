What does blood deliver in the body?(This was the surprise question)

(A) ** Oxygen, hormones, and nutrients

(B) Oxygen, hormones, and bones

(C) Bones, oxygen, and nutrients

(D) Hormonesss Oxyogen, and nutrienents

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Oxygen, nutrients and hormones are delivered around the body in the blood and carbon dioxide and other waste products are removed.

Concept note-2: -Blood brings oxygen and nutrients to all the parts of the body so they can keep working. Blood carries carbon dioxide and other waste materials to the lungs, kidneys, and digestive system to be removed from the body.

Concept note-3: -The heart, blood and blood vessels work together to service the cells of the body. Using the network of arteries, veins and capillaries, blood carries carbon dioxide to the lungs (for exhalation) and picks up oxygen. From the small intestine, the blood gathers food nutrients and delivers them to every cell.

Concept note-4: -These nutrients include glucose, amino acids, vitamins, minerals, and fatty acids. Blood also transports some hormones secreted by endocrine system glands to target organs and tissues.

Concept note-5: -All red blood cells contain a red pigment known as hemoglobin. Oxygen binds to hemoglobin, and is transported around the body in that way. In tiny blood vessels in the lung, the red blood cells pick up oxygen from inhaled (breathed in) air and carry it through the bloodstream to all parts of the body.
