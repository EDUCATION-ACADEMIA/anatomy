GENERAL ANATOMY

CARDIOVASCULAR SYSTEM

[SOURCES]
The circulatory system works with the ____ system to obtain energy from nutrients that we eat.

(A) respiratory

(B) ** digestive

(C) skeletal

(D) nervous

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Digestive System works with Circulatory System to obtain energy from the food we eat.

Concept note-2: -The small intestine absorbs most of the nutrients in your food, and your circulatory system passes them on to other parts of your body to store or use. Special cells help absorbed nutrients cross the intestinal lining into your bloodstream.

Concept note-3: -As food is digested, blood flows through the intestinal capillaries and picks up nutrients, such as glucose (sugar), vitamins, and minerals. These nutrients are delivered to the body tissues by the blood. Kidneys and urinary system.

Concept note-4: -The capillaries have very thin walls, and through them, nutrients and oxygen are delivered to the cells. Waste products are brought into the capillaries.

Concept note-5: -Digesting food requires teamwork between the digestive system and several other organ systems, including the nervous, cardiovascular, and muscular systems. When you eat a meal, the organs of the digestive system need more blood to perform their digestive functions.
