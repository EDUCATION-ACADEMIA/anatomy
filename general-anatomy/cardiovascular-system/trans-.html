Trans-

(A) ** across

(B) in; into

(C) out

(D) near

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -trans-prefix. : on or to the other side of : across : beyond. transatlantic. : so or such as to change in form or position or transfer.

Concept note-2: -Trans-(prefix): From the Latin meaning “across, over, or beyond.” Medical terms containing “trans-“ as a prefix include transcription, transfusion, transplant, transsexual, transurethral, transvaginal, transvestism, etc.

Concept note-3: -Prefix. trans-Extending across, through, or over.

Concept note-4: -transportation: act of carrying ‘across’ transfer: carry ‘across’ translucent: of light going ‘across’ transparent: of light going fully ‘across’ translate: carry one language ‘across’ to another. transatlantic: ‘across’ the Atlantic Ocean. transit: a going ‘across’ More items
