What does blood carry AWAY that the cells produce?

(A) arteries

(B) platelets

(C) veins

(D) ** waste

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Blood carries carbon dioxide and other waste materials to the lungs, kidneys, and digestive system to be removed from the body.

Concept note-2: -Oxygen, nutrients and hormones are delivered around the body in the blood and carbon dioxide and other waste products are removed.

Concept note-3: -Removing waste from the blood Two examples of the most common waste products are creatinine and urea. Creatinine is a waste product formed as a result of normal muscle activity. Urea is the by-product of protein and amino acid breakdown.
