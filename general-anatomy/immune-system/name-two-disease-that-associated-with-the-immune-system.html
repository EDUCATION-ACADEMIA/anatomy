Concept note-1: -Some types of cancer can change the way the immune system blood cells work. For instance, lymphomas (Hodgkin and non-Hodgkin), multiple myeloma, and most types of leukemia start in immune system blood cells.

Concept note-2: -The immune system and cancer Lymphomas are classified as Hodgkin lymphoma and non-Hodgkin lymphoma. A type of cancer that starts in the blood-forming tissue in the bone marrow. It causes large numbers of abnormal white blood cells to be produced and enter the bloodstream.
