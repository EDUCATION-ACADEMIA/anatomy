Treatment for illness caused by bacteria.

(A) Rest, plenty of fluids

(B) ** Antibiotics prescribed by Doctor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Antibiotics are medications that destroy or slow down the growth of bacteria. Doctors prescribe them to treat bacterial infections. They do this by killing bacteria and preventing them from multiplying. They include a range of powerful drugs used to treat diseases caused by bacteria.

Concept note-2: -Antibiotics are used to treat or prevent some types of bacterial infection. They kill bacteria or prevent them from reproducing and spreading. Antibiotics arenâ€™t effective against viral infections. This includes the common cold, flu, most coughs and sore throats.

Concept note-3: -Treatment for Antibiotic-Resistant Infections NYU Langone experts treat antibiotic-resistant infections with medications that target the bacteria causing the infection. Sometimes they prescribe a combination of medications. Our doctors prescribe different antibiotics based on the type of antibiotic-resistant infection.
