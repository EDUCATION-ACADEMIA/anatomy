GENERAL ANATOMY

IMMUNE SYSTEM

[SOURCES]
What might happen if your immune system didn’t recognize a germ that has invaded your body?

(A) ** germ would have time to multiply & make you sick

(B) WBCs will begin killing healthy cells instead

(C) antibodies will begin attacking each other

(D) body will stop producing WBCs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Your immune system is your body’s defense against infections and other harmful invaders. Without it, you would constantly get sick from bacteria or viruses.

Concept note-2: -An autoimmune disorder occurs when the body’s immune system attacks and destroys healthy body tissue by mistake.

Concept note-3: -How Does the Immune System Work? When the body senses foreign substances (called antigens), the immune system works to recognize the antigens and get rid of them. B lymphocytes are triggered to make antibodies (also called immunoglobulins). These proteins lock onto specific antigens.

Concept note-4: -When your immune system is exposed to a new germ for the first time, it responds by trying to fight it off-which means you may become sick. But afterward, the immune cells will remember the invader and be better equipped to fight it, should it return.
