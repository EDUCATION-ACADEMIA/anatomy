Virus-infected cell secrete a protein named interferon, this protein will alert neighboring cells of the virus, these cell will produce antivirus in them to help prevent viruses infections.

(A) Chemical Barriers

(B) Skin

(C) ** Interferon

(D) Component Proteins

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Interferon is secreted by cells in response to stimulation by a virus or other foreign substance, but it does not directly inhibit the virus’s multiplication. Rather, it stimulates the infected cells and those nearby to produce proteins that prevent the virus from replicating within them.

Concept note-2: -Interferons (IFNs)-the body’s first line of antiviral defence-are cytokines that are secreted by host cells in response to virus infection.

Concept note-3: -Reason: Interferons protect the non-infected cells from bacterial infection. Assertion is true but Reason is false.

Concept note-4: -Virus Alert Interferons are secreted by infected cells to warn their neighbors, and once stimulated, cells of the immune system secrete interferons as part of their viral surveillance. Interferons are small proteins that bind to receptors on the cell surface.

Concept note-5: -Interferons are antiviral proteins.
