If you are feeling ill and the doctor prescribes antibiotics to help you feel better, it means you are infected with ____

(A) Virus

(B) ** Bacteria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Antibiotics are used to treat or prevent some types of bacterial infection. They work by killing bacteria or preventing them from spreading. But they do not work for everything. Many mild bacterial infections get better on their own without using antibiotics.

Concept note-2: -Antibiotics are used to treat or prevent some types of bacterial infection. They kill bacteria or prevent them from reproducing and spreading. Antibiotics arenâ€™t effective against viral infections. This includes the common cold, flu, most coughs and sore throats.

Concept note-3: -Antibiotics are medicines that fight infections caused by bacteria in humans and animals by either killing the bacteria or making it difficult for the bacteria to grow and multiply.

Concept note-4: -Antibiotics Reduce Inflammation Many processes that make a person feel miserable are related to inflammatory responses. The Common Cold causes inflammation in the nasopharyngeal tract.

Concept note-5: -Bacterial infections are treated with antibiotics such as amoxicillin, erythromycin and ciprofloxacin. There are many different types of antibiotic, with different ways of working; the choice depends on the type of infection you have.
