The liver has only one function:making the bile

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bile is a fluid that is made and released by the liver and stored in the gallbladder.

Concept note-2: -Your liver continually produces bile. This is a chemical that helps turn fats into energy that your body uses. Bile is necessary for the digestive process. Your liver also creates albumin.

Concept note-3: -Bile is produced in your liver and stored in your gallbladder. Eating a meal that contains even a small amount of fat signals your gallbladder to release bile, which flows through a small tube into the upper part of your small intestine (duodenum).

Concept note-4: -So, considering the statement asked we can say that the bile is necessary for fat digestion, it is stored in gallbladder but it is not only important for normal sugar digestion it has other functions also. So, from above discussion it can be concluded that the correct answer is option C.
