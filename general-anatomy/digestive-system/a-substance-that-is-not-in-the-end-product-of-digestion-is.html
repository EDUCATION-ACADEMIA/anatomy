A substance that is not in the end product of digestion is:

(A) Glucose

(B) Glycerol

(C) Amino acids

(D) ** Maltose

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maltose and isomaltose are the disaccharides (glucose-glucose) produced as end products of starch digestion. The diet also may contain lactose (galactose-glucose) and sucrose (fructose-glucose).

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: a. polysaccharides are NOT an end-product of digestion.

Concept note-3: -Maltase-The substrate of maltase is maltose which when carried further in the process gives glucose as the final product or end product.

Concept note-4: -Maltase is synthesised by the human body in the cells of the intestinal mucosa, but also by plant cells, bacteria and yeast. Maltase is a digestive enzyme that is involved in the breakdown of sugars and their assimilation by the body.
