Concept note-2: -Receptors in your skin send signals back to the hypothalamus to modify your core body temperature. The hypothalamus then notifies the brain to start a process called vasoconstriction to combat the cold air that surrounds you. The muscles around your blood vessels tighten to reduce blood flow to your extremities.
