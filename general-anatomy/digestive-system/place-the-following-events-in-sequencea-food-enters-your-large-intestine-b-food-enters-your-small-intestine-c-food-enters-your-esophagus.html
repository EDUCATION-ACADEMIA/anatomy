Place the following events in sequence:A) Food enters your large intestine; B) Food enters your small intestine; C) Food enters your esophagus

(A) A, B, C

(B) ** C, B, A

(C) C, A, B

(D) B, A, C

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Final stage-The correct sequence of the human alimentary canal is Mouth → Oesophagus → Stomach → Small intestine → Large intestine.

Concept note-2: -Material passes through the ascending, transverse, descending and sigmoid portions of the colon, and finally into the rectum. From the rectum, the waste is expelled from the body.

Concept note-3: -Figure 2: The digestive processes are ingestion, propulsion, mechanical digestion, chemical digestion, absorption, and defecation.

Concept note-4: -What is the correct order that a bolus of food will pass through the small intestine? Explanation: The correct order that the food will pass as is first the duodenum, then the jejunum and finally the ileum.

Concept note-5: -After you swallow, peristalsis pushes the food down your esophagus into your stomach. Stomach. Glands in your stomach lining make stomach acid and enzymes that break down food. Muscles of your stomach mix the food with these digestive juices.
