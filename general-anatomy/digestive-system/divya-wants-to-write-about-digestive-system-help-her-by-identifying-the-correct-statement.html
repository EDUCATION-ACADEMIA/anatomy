GENERAL ANATOMY

DIGESTIVE SYSTEM

[SOURCES]
Divya wants to write about digestive system help her by identifying the correct statement.

(A) Digestive system help us to visit Goa.

(B) ** The organs that help us to digest food together form a digestive system.

(C) Digestive system is the way to help the electricity to come.

(D) Digestive system help us to perform on the dance stage.

(E) All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The organs of the digestive system are the mouth, esophagus, stomach, pancreas, liver, gallbladder, small intestine, large intestine and anus. Recognizing how these organs work together to digest food is key to understanding how digestion works. The digestive process begins in the mouth.

Concept note-2: -The GI tract is a series of hollow organs joined in a long, twisting tube from the mouth to the anus. The hollow organs that make up the GI tract are the mouth, esophagus, stomach, small intestine, large intestine, and anus.

Concept note-3: -The main organs that make up the digestive system (in order of their function) are the mouth, esophagus, stomach, small intestine, large intestine, rectum and anus. Helping them along the way are the pancreas, gall bladder and liver.

Concept note-4: -esophagus. stomach (and duodenum) small intestine (or small bowel) colon (or large intestine/bowel) and rectum.
