EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A genome is the complete set of genetic information in an organism. It provides all of the information the organism requires to function. In living organisms, the genome is stored in long molecules of DNA called chromosomes.

Concept note-2: -In humans, genes vary in size from a few hundred DNA bases to more than 2 million bases. An international research effort called the Human Genome Project, which worked to determine the sequence of the human genome and identify the genes that it contains, estimated that humans have between 20, 000 and 25, 000 genes.

Concept note-3: -In the fields of molecular biology and genetics, a genome is all the genetic information of an organism. It consists of nucleotide sequences of DNA (or RNA in RNA viruses).

Concept note-4: -Humans typically have 23 pairs of chromosomes, or 46 chromosomes in total. Chromosomes are made up of long strands of DNA, which contain all the bodyâ€™s genes.

Concept note-5: -Definition. Gene is a segment of DNA that gives instruction for the synthesis of gene products such as RNA or protein. The whole nuclear DNA of a cell in an organism comprises its genome. It is the basic unit of heredity. It includes all the coding and non-coding DNA, along with mitochondrial and chloroplast DNA.
