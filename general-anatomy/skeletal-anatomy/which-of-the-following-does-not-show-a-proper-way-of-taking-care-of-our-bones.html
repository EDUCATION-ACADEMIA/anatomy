Which of the following DOES NOT show a proper way of taking care of our bones?

(A) drinking milk and eating foods rich in calcium

(B) having enough rest and proper exercise

(C) wearing protective gears in playing physical sports

(D) ** immobility prevents you from having an accident

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apply pressure to the wound with a sterile bandage, a clean cloth or a clean piece of clothing. Immobilize the injured area. Don’t try to realign the bone or push a bone that’s sticking out back in.

Concept note-2: -Include plenty of calcium in your diet. Pay attention to vitamin D. Your body needs vitamin D to absorb calcium. Include physical activity in your daily routine. Avoid substance abuse.

Concept note-3: -Get just enough calcium. Calcium is the building block of bones. Consider a vitamin D supplement. Vitamin D is also critical for bone health. Get plenty of exercise. Know your individual risk. Give your bones a check-up. 04-Nov-2013

Concept note-4: -Live a sedentary lifestyle. Consume insufficient amounts of calcium and vitamin D. Drink excessive amounts of alcohol. Make poor dietary decisions. Smoke cigarettes. 29-Dec-2015
