Your feet and hands contain more than half of the amount of bones in your body!

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -More than half of the bones in your body are found in your hands and feet. There are 27 in each hand and 26 in each foot, and the way that they are arranged is remarkably similar. If you bend your fingers, you can see that they each contain three bones, except for your thumbs, which only have two.

Concept note-2: -More than half of your bones are in your hands and your feet. 106 of them to be exact. There are 54 bones in your hands, fingers and wrists. 26 bones in the human foot.

Concept note-3: -The hands and feet contain over half of the body’s bones. That is, the hands and feet contain more than half of the bones in your entire body.

Concept note-4: -Total 54 + 52 =106 which is more than 50% of human bones.

Concept note-5: -Approximately 25% of the bones in the body are found in our feet. These bones can be grouped into three parts: Tarsal Bones: a set of seven irregularly shaped bones of the midfoot that form the foot’s arch.
