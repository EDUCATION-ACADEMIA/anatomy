URINARY SYSTEM

[SOURCES]
Son

(A) Measure

(B) Ketone

(C) Stone

(D) ** Sound

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The sound of the sun was considered by most ancient religions as the most sacred sound of the universe. It is a sound that cannot be heard by the human ear, because the human ear only hears between 20-20, 000 Hertz. The sound of the sun is much lower in frequency and also much higher in frequency than we can hear. Q.

Concept note-2: -Today, we can hear the Sun’s movement-all of its waves, loops and eruptions-with our own ears. This sound helps scientists study what can’t be observed with the naked eye.

Concept note-3: -“When anything material moves, waves travel through it, and the same thing happens inside the Sun, ” he says in a NASA recording. Since humans can’t see that sort of movement on the sun with the naked eye, this “Sun song” is that movement translated into a medium we can actually sense – sound.

Concept note-4: -The biggest stars make the lowest, deepest sounds, like tubas and double basses. Small stars have high-pitched voices, like celestial flutes. These virtuosos don’t just play one “note” at a time, either-our own Sun has thousands of different sound waves bouncing around inside it at any given moment.
