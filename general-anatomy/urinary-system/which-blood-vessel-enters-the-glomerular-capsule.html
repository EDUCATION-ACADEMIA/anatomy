Which blood vessel enters the glomerular capsule?

(A) ** The afferent arteriole

(B) The efferent arteriole

(C) The peritubular capillaries

(D) The vasa recta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the kidney, the glomerulus represents the initial location of the renal filtration of blood. Blood enters the glomerulus through the afferent arteriole at the vascular pole, undergoes filtration in the glomerular capillaries, and exits the glomerulus through the efferent arteriole at the vascular pole.

Concept note-2: -Final answer: Blood enters the glomerulus through its afferent arteriole and leaves the glomerulus through its efferent arteriole.

Concept note-3: -So, the correct answer is ‘Afferent arteriole’.

Concept note-4: -Blood enters the glomerulus through afferent arteriole; The glomerulus is responsible for filtration of the blood.
