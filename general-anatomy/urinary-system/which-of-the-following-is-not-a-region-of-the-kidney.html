Which of the following is not a region of the kidney?

(A) cortex

(B) medulla

(C) pelvis

(D) ** hypothalamus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer and Explanation: (C) Postural reflexes are NOT a function of the hypothalamus.

Concept note-2: -Landmarks defining the regions of the hypothalamus include the lamina terminalis, pituitary gland, mammillary bodies, and superior hypothalamic sulcus. The hypothalamus is a bilateral collection of nuclei divided into three zones surrounding the third ventricle and the mammillary bodies.

Concept note-3: -Your hypothalamus, a structure deep in your brain, acts as your bodyâ€™s smart control coordinating center. Its main function is to keep your body in a stable state called homeostasis. It does its job by directly influencing your autonomic nervous system or by managing hormones.

Concept note-4: -Mammillary region. Relay station for reflexes related to the sense of smell. Tuberal region. Synthesis of hypothalamic regulating hormones. Supraoptic region. Synthesis & transport of oxytocin & ADH to the posterior pituitary gland. Preoptic region.
