EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This pair of purplish-brown organs is located below the ribs toward the middle of the back. Their function is to: Remove waste products and drugs from the body.

Concept note-2: -The kidneys are under the ribcage in the back, one on each side. Each adult kidney is about the size of a fist. Each kidney has an outer layer called the cortex, which contains filtering units. The center part of the kidney, the medulla (pronounced: meh-DUH-luh), has fan-shaped structures called pyramids.

Concept note-3: -There are two kidneys, one on each side of the backbone, above the waist. The kidneys remove waste and extra water from the blood and make urine. They also help keep chemicals balanced in the body, make hormones that control blood pressure, and stimulate bone marrow to make red blood cells.

Concept note-4: -Kidney pain vs back pain In comparison, kidney pain is typically located higher on your back and it often feels deeper. Most of the time, kidney pain symptoms occur under your ribs, to the right or left of your spine. Kidney pain may also radiate to other areas, such as your abdomen or groin.
