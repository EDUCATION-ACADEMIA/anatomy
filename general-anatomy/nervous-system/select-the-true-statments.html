select the true statments

(A) ** The central nervous system is made up of the brain and spinal cord.

(B) the neurons are the cns of our body.

(C) The peripheral nervous system is made up of nerves that branch off from the spinal cord and extend to all parts of the body.

(D) the brain is not the main part because its in the central nervous system which makes it different that the others.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The central nervous system is made up of the brain and spinal cord: The brain controls how we think, learn, move, and feel. The spinal cord carries messages back and forth between the brain and the nerves that run throughout the body.

Concept note-2: -The nervous system has two main parts: The central nervous system is made up of the brain and spinal cord. The peripheral nervous system is made up of nerves that branch off from the spinal cord and extend to all parts of the body.

Concept note-3: -The brain and spinal cord make up the central nervous system (CNS).

Concept note-4: -Complete answer: This statement is true that the central nervous system consists of the brain and spinal cord. Brain plays a central role in the control of most bodily functions and it includes bodily functions like movements, sensations, thoughts, speech, awareness and memory.
