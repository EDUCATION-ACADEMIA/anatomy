Which of the following is not a part of the Motor (Efferent) Division?

(A) ** Sensory Divison

(B) Parasympathetic Division

(C) Somatic Division

(D) Autonomic Division

(E) Sympathetic Division

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is (d), sensory fibers. The peripheral nervous system contains two sections as well: autonomic and somatic.

Concept note-2: -The motor division is also divided into two parts, the somatic nervous system and the autonomic nervous system. The somatic nervous system carries messages that control body movements. It is responsible for activities that are under your control, such as waving your hand or kicking a ball.

Concept note-3: -Finally, the efferent or motor division is again subdivided into the somatic nervous system and the autonomic nervous system.

Concept note-4: -Which of the following is NOT part of the sensory division? dendrites.

Concept note-5: -The autonomic nervous system is divided into three parts: the sympathetic nervous system, the parasympathetic nervous system and the enteric nervous system.
