Concept note-1: -Word forms: plural anglers. countable noun. An angler is someone who fishes with a fishing rod as a hobby. Synonyms: fisherman or woman, fisher, piscator or piscatrix More Synonyms of angler.

Concept note-2: -The five basic methods of angling are bait fishing, fly-fishing, bait casting, spinning, and trolling. All are used in both freshwater and saltwater angling. Bait fishing, also called still fishing or bottom fishing, is certainly the oldest and most universally used method.

Concept note-3: -Fishing gives you the opportunity to form special bonds with family and friends. Adventures on the water bring people together in a number of ways. When you share the thrill of a first catch or the stunning scenery of a new waterway, you form bonds with your fishing buddies that can last a lifetime.

Concept note-4: -My hobby is gardening, reading books, learning scientific facts, singing, dancing, pampering babies, etc. Hobbies are activities that relax the mind when we are fully exhausted. I chose these activities as my hobby as they help me to cool down and I feel relieved.
