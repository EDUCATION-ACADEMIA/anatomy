Which of the following are not examples of renewable resources?

(A) ** Coal and natural gas

(B) Plants and animals

(C) Water and sunlight

(D) Wind and soil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nonrenewable energy resources include coal, natural gas, oil, and nuclear energy. Once these resources are used up, they cannot be replaced, which is a major problem for humanity as we are currently dependent on them to supply most of our energy needs.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Nuclear minerals. Wind, Biogas/Biofuels, Sunlight and Natural Gas are all renewable resources while Fossil fuels like coal and petroleum, Nuclear minerals are non-renewable resources of energy.

Concept note-3: -Fossil energy sources, including oil, coal and natural gas, are non-renewable resources that formed when prehistoric plants and animals died and were gradually buried by layers of rock.

Concept note-4: -Fossil fuels-coal, oil and gas-on the other hand, are non-renewable resources that take hundreds of millions of years to form.

Concept note-5: -Technology – Building physical products such as smartphones, computers, tablets, and other modern electric devices is classified as a tangible asset. Oil and Gas Owning a considerable amount of tangible assets that require large amounts of capital to finance.
