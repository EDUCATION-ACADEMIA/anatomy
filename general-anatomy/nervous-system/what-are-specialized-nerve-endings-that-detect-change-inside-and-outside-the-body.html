What are specialized nerve endings that detect change inside and outside the body?

(A) interneurons

(B) ** receptors

(C) motor neurons

(D) reflex arc

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Somatic nervous system Sensory receptors are specialized endings of nerve fibers that detect information in and around the body.

Concept note-2: -Nociceptors are specialized nerve endings that respond directly to noxious stimuli and indirectly to chemicals released from damaged tissue, such as bradykinin, histamine, potassium, acidity, ATP, acetylcholine, serotonin, substance P, and other peptides.

Concept note-3: -Ruffini corpuscles in skin are considered to be skin stretch sensitive receptors of the discriminative touch system. They also work with the proprioceptors in joints and muscles to indicate the position and movement of body parts.

Concept note-4: -Thermoreceptors detect changes in temperature. Mechanoreceptors detect mechanical forces. Photoreceptors detect light during vision. More specific examples of sensory receptors are baroreceptors, propioceptors, hygroreceptors, and osmoreceptors.
