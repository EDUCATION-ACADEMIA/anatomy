Do males and females have different endocrine system organs?

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The endocrine glands are the same in both male and females except for the reproductive glands, the testes in males and ovaries in females.

Concept note-2: -The female ovaries, male testes, and pituitary, thyroid, and adrenal glands are major constituents of the endocrine system.

Concept note-3: -The testis, an endocrine gland that secretes testosterone in large quantities, is present in males but not in females.

Concept note-4: -In humans, all of the endocrine glands in the body are very similar in size and shape between males and females. These glands include the pineal gland, the hypothalamus, the pituitary gland, the thyroid gland, the thymus gland, the pancreas, and the adrenal glands.
