This disease is when low blood glucose or low blood sugar occurs when blood glucose drops below healthy levels

(A) Giantism

(B) Diabetes

(C) Hypothyroidism

(D) ** Hypoglycemia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hypoglycemia is a condition in which your blood sugar (glucose) level is lower than the standard range. Glucose is your body’s main energy source. Hypoglycemia is often related to diabetes treatment. But other drugs and a variety of conditions-many rare-can cause low blood sugar in people who don’t have diabetes.

Concept note-2: -More severe symptoms, such as unclear thinking or seizure, occur when the blood sugar is much lower (less than 40 mg/dL or 2.2 mmol/L). Even if you do not have symptoms, your blood sugar could still be too low (called hypoglycemic unawareness).

Concept note-3: -Low blood glucose, also called low blood sugar or hypoglycemia, occurs when the level of glucose in your blood drops below what is healthy for you. For many people with diabetes, this means a blood glucose reading lower than 70 milligrams per deciliter (mg/dL).
