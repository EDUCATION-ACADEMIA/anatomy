This is a stage of sexual development during which a person becomes sexually mature and physically able to reproduce.

(A) Adolescence

(B) ** Puberty

(C) Menopause

(D) Teenage

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Puberty is the process of physical maturation where an adolescent reaches sexual maturity and becomes capable of reproduction. On average, puberty typically begins between 8 and 13 in females and 9 and 14 in males.

Concept note-2: -Puberty is the time in life when a boy or girl becomes sexually mature. It is a process that usually happens between ages 10 and 14 for girls and ages 12 and 16 for boys.

Concept note-3: -During adolescence (usually considered age 10 to the late teens or early 20s), boys and girls reach adult height and weight and undergo sexual maturation (puberty.

Concept note-4: -Penis, scrotum, and testes will grow. Ejaculation and nocturnal emissions (’wet dreams’) occur and sperm production begins or continues. Voice starts to crack and becomes deeper. May have swelling under the nipples (this growth usually goes away by the end of puberty).
