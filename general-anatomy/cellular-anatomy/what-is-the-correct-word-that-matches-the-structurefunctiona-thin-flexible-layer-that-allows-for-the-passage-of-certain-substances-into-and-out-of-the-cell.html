What is the correct word that matches the structure/function:a thin, flexible layer that allows for the passage of certain substances into and out of the cell

(A) ** Cell Membrane

(B) Cell Wall

(C) Cytoskeleton

(D) Cytoplasm

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The cell membrane, also called the plasma membrane, is found in all cells and separates the interior of the cell from the outside environment. The cell membrane consists of a lipid bilayer that is semipermeable. The cell membrane regulates the transport of materials entering and exiting the cell.

Concept note-2: -cell membrane, also called plasma membrane, thin membrane that surrounds every living cell, delimiting the cell from the environment around it.

Concept note-3: -The fluid mosaic model is a way of describing the structure of cell membranes. The model likens the membrane to a “mosaic” of different components, consisting of a fluid or elastic double layer made up of lipid molecules and large protein molecules.

Concept note-4: -The plasma membrane is a flexible, lipid bilayer that surrounds and contains the cytoplasm of the cell.
