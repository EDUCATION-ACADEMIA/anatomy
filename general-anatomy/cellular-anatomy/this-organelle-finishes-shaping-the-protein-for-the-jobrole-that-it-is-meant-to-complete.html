This organelle finishes shaping the protein for the job/role that it is meant to complete.

(A) ER

(B) Nucleus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What Does the Golgi Apparatus Do? The Golgi apparatus functions as a molecular assembly line in which membrane proteins undergo extensive post-translational modification.

Concept note-2: -The Golgi apparatus, or Golgi complex, functions as a factory in which proteins received from the ER are further processed and sorted for transport to their eventual destinations: lysosomes, the plasma membrane, or secretion.

Concept note-3: -Golgi apparatus is often regarded as the secretary organelle of the cell. This is because it helps in transporting protein within or outside the cell.It can either be transported from inside to outside or within the cell region.It is mainly used for the transportation of protein outside the cell.

Concept note-4: -The endoplasmic reticulum can either be smooth or rough, and in general its function is to produce proteins for the rest of the cell to function.
