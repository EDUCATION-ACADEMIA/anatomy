How are you doing today?

(A) ** Great!

(B) ** Okay

(C) ** Meh

(D) ** I’ve been better

(E) ** I’m not doing my best today

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Say “I’m okay, thanks” if you aren’t feeling well but want to be polite. If you are feeling ill or a little sick, you can use this response to let the person know this in a polite way. The person can then move on with the conversation or ask you more probing questions.

Concept note-2: -"I don’t feel well.” “I am feeling sick.” “I am unwell."

Concept note-3: -I’m fine, thanks. How about you? Good, thanks. And you? I’m good. And yourself? Not bad. How are you? Fine, and you? I’m doing well, and you? Good, how about you? 30-Sept-2022
