How many times does an average person breathe in a minute?

(A) ** 12

(B) 18

(C) 27

(D) 35

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Respiration rates may increase with fever, illness, and other medical conditions. When checking respiration, it is important to also note whether a person has any difficulty breathing. Normal respiration rates for an adult person at rest range from 12 to 16 breaths per minute.

Concept note-2: -A normal breathing rate for an adult at rest is 8 to 16 breaths per minute.

Concept note-3: -Breathing is something that we all do without usually realizing it. We breathe in and out about 22, 000 times a day.
