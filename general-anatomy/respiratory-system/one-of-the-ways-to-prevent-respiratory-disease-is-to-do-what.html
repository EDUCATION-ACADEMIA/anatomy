One of the ways to prevent respiratory disease is to do what?

(A) ** Vaccinate animals.

(B) Keep animals inside as much as possible.

(C) Give animals additional oxygen.

(D) Check each animal’s respiratory rate daily.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Avoid Exposure to Air Pollutants That Can Damage Your Lungs Secondhand smoke, chemicals in the home and workplace, mold and radon all can cause or worsen lung disease. You can take steps to improve your indoor air quality.

Concept note-2: -Influenza – to protect against season flu each year. Tdap – to protect against tetanus, diphtheria and pertussis (whooping cough) Pneumococcal pneumonia – to protect against the most common type of bacterial pneumonia. More items •07-Feb-2023

Concept note-3: -Polio. Tetanus. Flu (Influenza) Hepatitis B. Hepatitis A. Rubella. Hib. Measles. More items •15-Sept-2022
