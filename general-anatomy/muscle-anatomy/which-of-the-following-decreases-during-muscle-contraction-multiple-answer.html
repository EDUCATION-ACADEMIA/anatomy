Which of the following decreases during muscle contraction? (Multiple answer)

(A) ** I band

(B) A band

(C) Z line

(D) ** H zone

(E) M line

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When muscle contracts, The Z discs come close together. The width of the I bands decreases. The width of the H zones decreases.

Concept note-2: -During muscle contraction, the I band reduces in size as the overlap between the thick filaments and the thin filaments increases. The H zone also decreases in length because of the same reason.

Concept note-3: -The H zone-the central region of the A zone-contains only thick filaments and is shortened during contraction. The I band contains only thin filaments and also shortens.

Concept note-4: -When muscle contracts, the H zone (central region of Azone) which consists of thick filaments is shortened and the I band which contains only thin filaments is also shortened during the time of contraction.

Concept note-5: -So, the correct answer is ‘Decrease in length of actin myofilaments’.
