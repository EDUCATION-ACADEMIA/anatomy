Which of the following nerves can cause the skeletal muscle to contract?(Bonus recall lesson semalam)

(A) Autonomic

(B) ** Somatic

(C) Sympathetic

(D) Parasympathetic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Somatic Nervous System☆ The somatic nervous system provides control of skeletal muscle movement. Conscious control of movement originates in the motor cortex (both premotor and primary motor cortex).

Concept note-2: -The somatic nervous system, also called the somatomotor or somatic efferent nervous system, supplies motor impulses to the skeletal muscles. Because these nerves permit conscious control of the skeletal muscles, it is sometimes called the voluntary nervous system.

Concept note-3: -Your somatic nervous system allows you to move and control muscles throughout your body. It also feeds information from four of your senses-smell, sound, taste and touch-into your brain.

Concept note-4: -The sensory-somatic nervous system is composed of cranial and spinal nerves and contains both sensory and motor neurons. Sensory neurons transmit sensory information from the skin, skeletal muscle, and sensory organs to the central nervous system (CNS).
