What is the first paragraph of the constitution called?

(A) introduction

(B) articles

(C) ** preamble

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: Preamble is called the preface of Indian Constitution because of the following reasons: The Preamble of the constitution contains the philosophy on which the entire constitution is built.

Concept note-2: -The Preamble of the Indian Constitution begins with “We, The People Of India”.

Concept note-3: -The first page of the Indian Constitution is known as the Preamble.

Concept note-4: -Solution: The Preamble is the introduction or the preface to the constitution. It lays down the basic principles and objectives of the Constitution.
