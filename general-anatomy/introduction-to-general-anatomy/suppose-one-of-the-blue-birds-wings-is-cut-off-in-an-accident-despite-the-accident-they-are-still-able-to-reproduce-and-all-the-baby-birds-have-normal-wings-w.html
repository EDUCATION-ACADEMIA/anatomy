Suppose one of the blue bird’s wings is cut off in an accident. Despite the accident, they are still able to reproduce and all the baby birds have normal wings. Which explanation best describes why?

(A) The red bird wings aredominant to the blue bird wings.

(B) The blue bird wings aredominant to the red bird wings.

(C) Cutting the wing of thebird changed its genes.

(D) ** Cutting the wing of thebird did not change its genes.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Wing clipping is the process of trimming a bird’s primary wing feathers or remiges so that it is not fully flight-capable, until it moults, sheds the cut feathers and grows new ones.

Concept note-2: -To hinder flight, you must trim the primary feathers. Some people choose to trim a varying number of the outermost ten primary feathers, but only the outermost five primary feathers need to be trimmed to prevent flight, in most cases.

Concept note-3: -Wings need to be clipped typically every 1-3 months after the start of a molt cycle, as new feathers grow back. However, every bird is different; some need clipping more often and some less.

Concept note-4: -The thunder, lightning, rain and wind blew down many trees. A big, heavy branch hit their nest and the mother bird was killed. The strong wind blew away the two baby birds to the other side of the forest and were separated by a little distance.
