The Brazilian rain forest is an area with significantbiodiversity. As the rain forest is replaced with agricultural land, it is reasonable to predict a reduction in-

(A) consumption of solar energy.

(B) ** sustainability over time.

(C) precipitation levels.

(D) average daily temperature.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Greater biodiversity in ecosystems, species, and individuals leads to greater stability. For example, species with high genetic diversity and many populations that are adapted to a wide variety of conditions are more likely to be able to weather disturbances, disease, and climate change.

Concept note-2: -The ocean ecosystem has a high sustainability because of-the large variety of different organisms.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: Biodiversity is the main factor that contributes to the sustainability of an ecosystem.

Concept note-4: -The greater a habitat’s biodiversity, the greater will be that habitat’s-sustainability over time with varying conditions.
