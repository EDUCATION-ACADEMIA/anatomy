The toes would ____ to the knees?

(A) ** Inferior

(B) Superior

(C) Anterior

(D) Posterior

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The foot is distal to the knee. The foot can be described as inferior to the knee but the term that best fits the comparison refers to the relation of the foot to the knee as part of the lower limb. The proximal end of the lower limb is the hip and the knee is distal to the hip but proximal to the foot.

Concept note-2: -In medicine, refers to a part of the body that is closer to the center of the body than another part. For example, the knee is proximal to the toes. The opposite is distal.

Concept note-4: -The knee is proximal to the foot. Moving distally from the hip brings you to the thigh. The foot is distal to the knee. The skin is superficial to bone.
