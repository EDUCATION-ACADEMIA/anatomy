Which one of the following is not one of the 4 main tissue types?

(A) Epithelial

(B) Connective

(C) ** Bone

(D) Muscles

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer and Explanation: Extracellular bone matrix (e) is not one of the four fundamental tissue types in the human body.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: C. Brain tissue is not one of the four basic body tissues.

Concept note-3: -There are 4 basic types of tissue: connective tissue, epithelial tissue, muscle tissue, and nervous tissue. Connective tissue supports other tissues and binds them together (bone, blood, and lymph tissues). Epithelial tissue provides a covering (skin, the linings of the various passages inside the body).

Concept note-4: -In the options provided for the question, the one that is not a type of tissue is embryonic. Embryonic is not a type of tissue; rather it refers to a state of a cell or an organism in the early developmental stages. The four tissue types are muscle, nervous, epithelial, and connective tissue.

Concept note-5: -The four types of tissues are exemplified in nervous tissue, stratified squamous epithelial tissue, cardiac muscle tissue, and connective tissue in small intestine.
