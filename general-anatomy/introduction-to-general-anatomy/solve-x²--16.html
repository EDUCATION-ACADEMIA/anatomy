Solve. x² = √16

(A) x = ± 2

(B) ** x = ± 4

(C) x = 4i

(D) x = 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The value of x² – 16 ÷ x + 4 is x-4.

Concept note-2: -If we have the equation x2 = 16, what are the solutions to the equation? Since the square of a positive or negative number are always positive, this equation has two solutions, namely x =-4 or x = 4.

Concept note-3: -The solutions to x2-6x = 16 are x =-2 and x = 8.
